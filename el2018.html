    [ Description ]

    ThugCrowd is doing a 24 hour CTF & Hacking Challenge Marathon to benefit
    the Extra Life charity. Proceeds will go towards supporting Children's 
    Miracle Network Hospitals. We will be streaming live on Twitch & Periscope.

    This stream will consist of each one of the participants working on a CTF
    or other hacking challenge in a timeslot of about an hour. You can work 
    in teams or by yourself. The only requirement is that you have the 
    ability to screenshare from at least one machine. 

    If you complete your challenge before the alloted time is over, then it 
    is encouraged that you spend some time after discussing what you found, 
    your techniques, and taking questions from the chat. 

    Alternatively, if you want to do a small demo or discussion of a tool,
    technique, PoC exploit, etc, we can schedule you a smaller slot as well. 

    We will be working on giveaways to do on the stream too for people who 
    donate, as well as other random events / challenges to occur during the
    duration of the stream. If you have any ideas, ping yuu~
